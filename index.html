Our Story

Forty years of family commitment.

EBerry Harvest Company is a family-owned farm labor contractor headquartered in LaBelle, Florida. For over four decades, founder Refugio "Cuco" Flores has built relationships with growers across the Southeast and beyond — earning trust one harvest at a time.

We manage H-2A workforces, housing, transportation, and payroll for client farms growing more than 40 specialty commodities. From citrus groves to nursery operations, from pumpkin fields to packing houses, our teams show up ready to work and ready to care.

Behind every paycheck we process is a worker, a family, a life. That's the standard we hold ourselves to.

What We Do

Full-service farm labor solutions.

From prep operations through post-harvest delivery, we handle every step of the agricultural workforce.

🌱

Prep Operations

Land preparation, planting, irrigation, and field readiness for every crop cycle.

🌾

Field Harvest

Skilled crews trained in food safety regulation compliance, working efficiently across every commodity we serve.

📦

Post-Harvest

Sorting, grading, and quality control to keep your product moving from field to market.

🥬

Packing & Repacking

Packing house labor and repacking services to specification, ready for the next leg of distribution.

Hauling & Transport

DOT-compliant hauling and worker transport across our six-state operating region.
